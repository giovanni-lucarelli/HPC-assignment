\section{Conclusion}

\begin{frame}{Conclusion}

\textbf{Key findings}
\begin{itemize}
  \item MPI communication time is small compared to the stencil update
  \item OpenMP efficiency decreases at high thread counts (likely memory-bandwidth / NUMA limited)
\end{itemize}

\textbf{Possible improvements}
\begin{itemize}
  \item Enable/verify SIMD vectorization of the stencil kernel
  \item Use MPI derived datatypes or optimized packing/unpacking
  \item Explore alternative schemes (e.g., higher-order stencils) and their performance/accuracy trade-offs
\end{itemize}

\end{frame}
